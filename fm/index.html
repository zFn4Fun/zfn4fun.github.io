Welcome to the sub-site dedicated to the Football Manager series

This is the place where I put all the stuff I make for Football Manager. You can access them by clicking on one of the rectangles below.

Currently there are 2 projects you can check out:

Hidden Attributes Guide

A small script based on the data provided by Nocious for FM12. It shows the potential value for 7 attributes that are hidden, based on the Personality, Media Handling Style and Determination of a player.

Understanding Player Roles Instructions

A collection of tables that show the available instructions for every role and duty.
